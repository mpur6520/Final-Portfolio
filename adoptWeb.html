LOVED PETS ADOPTION PROJECT

This project was challenging students to create a website pertaining to a fake client's needs. They had an adoption site and a list of requests for the student to take and produce, mixing both creativity and professionalism into the website-making world.


NOTES

The website is linked here. This was done SENIOR year.

REFLECTION

Through this project, I've improved on my understanding of javascript and implementing features to the site that I normally wouldn't. It forced me outside of my comfort zone and I'm proud of what I made.

STRUGGLES

Figuring out javascript was difficult because I set the high standards of both filtering the animals by their species, temperament, etc. as well as creating a carousel for the staff members. It was difficult but rewarding as I utilized the internet to help me understand.
