INFY APP CHALLENGE

The Infosys Foundation USA's "Infy App Challenge" is a competition that challenges students to design an app to address a social issue in their community. Together with three other classmates, my group designed "LeafFree", an app designed for recovering addicts.


NOTES

The website our team used is above, you can click through or alternatively go HERE to view in another tab. You can view each team members' personal pages using the navigation bar at the top with each students' name. This was done JUNIOR year.

REFLECTION

This app spanned across several months and was both challenging and a fun experience. I learned a lot about how to cater to users when designing an app that will be of use to them, solving issues our team was passionate about.

STRUGGLES

Preparing all the intricacies of the app were difficult considering there were many nuances, however we settled this by giving each person a niche role to ensure we got the project done on time and be proud of it.
