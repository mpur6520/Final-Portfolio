MARBLE MANIA

In my senior year Project & Prototyping class, our teacher introduced us to Unity, in which we would all follow a curated course to create two distinct games. One of these games was a marble race.


NOTES

To play, go here. To pause, press P. To start the race, press RETURN. To switch camera views, press C. This was done SENIOR year.

REFLECTION

Through this project, I've improved on my Unity and C# skills by being consistently introduced to aspects of the programming language I was unfamiliar with in the past. The process of designing the course was fun.

STRUGGLES

Due to my unfamiliarity with Unity, it was a struggle to navigate the panels at first. Once I got the hang of it and learned about new features, the process gradually became easier.
