GHOST SCHOOL FINAL PROJECT

The Emoticon competition was one where students would have to tackle one of three issues in an app or game format, those of which being recycling, greener communities, or the ocean. This is my entry, EcoMuncher.


NOTES

You can view a preview in the iframe, but you can also go HERE to view the actual website. This was done SOPHOMORE year of high school.

REFLECTION

I enjoyed the Ghost School project as a whole, particularly because I was super interested in RPG-like games at the time. The pixel-art style was nostalgic to me because of the type of games I grew up around, and so I wanted to put my best effort into a project that reflected that with the skills I had at the time.

STRUGGLES

I was focused on everything being perfectly aligned which led me to become frustrated and give up numerous times before tackling the issue again. This was a project where I learned perfection isn't imminent and I shouldn't chase it on my first attempt. I did what I could and I'm proud.
